# DApp PC Guide

A step-by-step guide to accessing and using the LinkLayerAI DApp on desktop.

### Step 1

Open the LinkLayerAI DApp 👉 (https://agent.linklayer.ai/)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/1.png)

### Step 2

Click "Log in" in the top right corner and connect your wallet (Note: Use a wallet address that holds Binance Alpha tokens)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/21.png)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/22.png)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/23.png)

### Step 3

After logging in, click your account address in the top right corner to view account information: wallet address, bound email, point balance, and referral count.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/31.png)

Click the point button in the top right corner to enter the Points Recharge interface.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/32.png)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/33.png)

Click "Earn" in the top right corner to copy your referral link with one click, or share directly via Twitter for promotion.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/34.png)

### Step 4

Alpha Agent: Click "Alpha" on the left side. The right panel will display all Binance Alpha token holdings for the current address. The traffic light indicates each token's liquidity health: Green = sufficient liquidity, Red = liquidity depletion risk, Yellow = transitional state.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/41.png)

Click "Explorer" to jump directly to the token's block explorer page. Click "Agent" to analyze the token's liquidity status.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/42.png)

### Step 5

Click "Insight" on the left, click "+", select "Binance", fill in your Binance read-only API Key and Secret Key, and confirm.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/51.png)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/52.png)

After successfully binding the Binance read-only API, the Binance icon will light up.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/53.png)

The bottom left corner shows the exchange spot list. Click any token icon and the Agent will provide real-time analysis and recommendations.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/54.png)

Perps Mining：

Retroactive：A one-time reward issued after you connect your API, automatically calculated based on your liquidation history from the past 6 months.

Recurring：Ongoing rewards distributed every 30 days, based on your new liquidation records during each period.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/55.png)

Perps Pilot: Click on your current perpetual positions in the exchange. The Agent will provide real-time analysis and recommendations for your perpetual positions.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/56.png)

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/57.png)

### Step 6

Click "Piloter" on the left. Select any token in "Picker" and the Agent can provide real-time analysis for spot or perpetual.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/61.png)

Enter the token name you want to analyze in the search box.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/62.png)

Click the ⭐️ in front of tokens you're interested in to pin them.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/63.png)

In "Tracker", tokens matching specific strategies will be filtered and displayed based on your selected strategy.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/64.png)

### Step 7

Click "Graph" on the left to view your referral graph.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/7.png)

### Step 8

Click "Rewards" on the left to swap LLA for LLAX and stake your tokens.

![](https://cdn.linklayer.ai/uploads/lladapp_use/8.png)
