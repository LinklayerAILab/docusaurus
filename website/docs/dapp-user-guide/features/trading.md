# Trading

Learn how to execute trades on the LinkLayerAI DApp.

## Trading Interface

The trading interface provides:

- **Order Book**: Real-time buy and sell orders
- **Price Charts**: Interactive charts with technical indicators
- **Order Forms**: Place market, limit, and stop orders
- **Position Management**: Monitor and manage active positions

## Order Types

### Market Orders

- Execute immediately at current market price
- Best for quick entries and exits
- May experience slippage in volatile markets

### Limit Orders

- Set a specific price for execution
- Provides price certainty
- May not execute if price doesn't reach target

### Stop-Loss Orders

- Automatically sell at a predetermined price
- Protects against significant losses
- Essential for risk management

### Take-Profit Orders

- Automatically sell at a target price
- Locks in profits
- Helps maintain trading discipline

## AI-Assisted Trading

### Alpha Agent

- Analyzes market trends and patterns
- Provides entry and exit signals
- Optimizes timing for trades

### Perps Agent

- Specialized for perpetual futures
- Manages leverage and funding rates
- Optimizes for perps-specific strategies

### Trading Agent

- Risk-adjusted position sizing

## Risk Management

### Setting Stop Loss

1. Determine your risk tolerance
2. Set stop-loss below entry for longs
3. Set stop-loss above entry for shorts
4. Adjust based on market volatility

### Position Sizing

- Never risk more than 1-2% per trade
- Use consistent position sizing rules
- Consider account balance and leverage

## Best Practices

1. **Always use stop-loss** on every trade
2. **Start with small positions** to test strategies
3. **Keep a trading journal** to track performance
4. **Stay updated** on market news and events
5. **Don't overtrade** - quality over quantity

## Troubleshooting

### Order Not Executed

- Check if price conditions are met
- Verify sufficient balance
- Ensure exchange connectivity

### Unexpected Slippage

- Reduce order size
- Use limit orders instead of market
- Check market liquidity

## Advanced Features

- **Trailing Stops**: Automatically adjust stop-loss as price moves favorably
- **Iceberg Orders**: Hide large orders
- **TWAP**: Time-weighted average price execution
- **Algo Trading**: Custom trading strategies
